FLYCOURIER OVERVIEW

Bajarang property, based in Delhi, is a privately held real estate investment firm that owns and manages 200 in real estate assets across the india. The firm is vertically integrated to have deep focus on acquisitions, asset management and property management. Bajarang is focused on the acquisition and management of multifamily, new development, and commercial office and retail assets. Since inception, we have acquired nearly 20,000 multifamily units, 1 million square feet of commercial office, hundreds of single family homes and 300k square feet of retail / mixed use.
